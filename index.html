LIVE A GHIBLI EXPERIENCE

Studio Ghibli (スタジオジブリ Sutajio Jiburi) is a Japanese animation studio, based in Tokyo. It was founded in 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, and to date, they have made more than 20 films, in addition to other types of work (short films, commercials, ...). They have the respect of the Japanese public and also the international one, where the critics and the animation sector show them a lot of respect.
